> Action

Unfortunately, the decision-making process for the rail yard site selection and approval has come and gone.

From 2012 to 2018, the school district, GoTriangle and the City/County government sytematically failed to inform parents and guardians at Creekside Elementary of the public zoning hearings and public comment periods for the rail yard site selection. As a result, almost all people with children at Creekside Elementary did not have a chance to participate in the site selection process.

On December 3, 2018, the Durham City Council voted to approve the industrial zone at Farrington Road and Ephesus Church Road.

There are steps you can take moving forward.
