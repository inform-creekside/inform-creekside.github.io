Traffic

  • Trains approaching the railyard operations maintenance facility (ROMF) from Chapel Hill will cross Farrington road near Wendell Road around 150 times a day, with road closures of 50 seconds for each crossing.

    • These temporary road closures to allow train crossings will cause cars to stop, creating back-ups for people approaching Creekside Elementary from Farrington Road and 54.

  • Over 100 employees will park at the ROMF industrial location, presumably on a daily basis.

  • During the construction phase starting in 2020, Farrington Road overpass will have temporary closures.

  • Farrington Road will never be widened from its current two-lane configuration.
