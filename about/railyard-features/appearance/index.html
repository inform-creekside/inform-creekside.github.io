Appearance

The site will be a rail yard, operations, and maintenance facility. As of the December 2018, there has been no full visual rendering of the appearance of the railyard from the road.

The site will include:

  • high voltage overhead electrical wire throughout the train storage area with poles supporting the wire
  • one acre operations and maintenance building
  • train tracks to park 20+ light rail trains
  • electrical substation the size of a mini mobile home
  • parking lot for more than 100 employees because this site will not be accessible by train.

To conceal these industrial elements from the roadway, GoTriangle has proposed adding a 4 foot berm and 8 foot ornamental trees spaced 16 feet apart. The site dimensions do not allow any larger berm. The poles and high voltage electrical wire will be visible from the road.
