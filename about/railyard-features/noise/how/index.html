How will this affect the school?

Based on written information on the railyard operations maintenance facility (ROMF) provided in 2018, no evidence supports the conclusion that noise will not be a problem for children at Creekside Elementary.

Absence of evidence does not mean evidence of absence.

Will there be noise at Creekside Elementary during the construction phase and during operation of the ROMF?

  • Based on the two noise reports issued in 2015 and 2018, the ROMF offers the potential to repeatedly emit noise over 60 dB, the current Durham City ordinance limit, more than 30 times a day.

  • At a meeting requested by Creekside parents in November 2018, GoTriangle confirmed that no noise testing had been done at Creekside Elementary.
